# Privacy Policy for Mythri

**Effective Date:** June 22, 2026

Welcome to **Mythri** ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your data when you use the Mythri AI-powered empathetic mental health companion (the "Service").

By using Mythri, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a highly personalized, empathetic, and culturally aware mental health companion, we collect the following types of information:

### A. Personal and Account Information
- **Account Data:** When you register, we may collect your username, email address, and an encrypted (hashed) password to secure your account.

### B. Conversation and Interaction Data
- **Text Conversations:** We store the chat history and context of your conversations with the Mythri AI to provide continuous and contextually aware support across sessions.
- **Voice Data:** If you use the voice features, we capture audio inputs. These audio inputs are temporarily processed via Speech-to-Text (STT) services to convert your speech into text.
- **Emotion and Sentiment Analysis:** Our systems analyze your text and voice inputs to detect emotional states and shifts in order to provide therapeutic, empathetic responses and detect potential crisis situations.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly to operate, maintain, and improve the Mythri service:
- **Service Delivery:** To power the AI conversations, process audio through STT/TTS pipelines, and maintain your session history.
- **Safety and Crisis Detection:** To run real-time crisis detection algorithms. If high-risk phrases indicating potential self-harm or danger are detected, the system will flag the conversation and provide immediate helpline resources.
- **Contextual Understanding:** To allow the "Neural Analyst" context engine to evaluate conversation history and provide culturally relevant, empathetic support.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

To provide state-of-the-art AI interactions, Mythri integrates with carefully selected third-party APIs. Your interaction data may be processed by:
- **Sarvam AI:** Used for high-accuracy Speech-to-Text (STT), emotionally expressive Text-to-Speech (TTS), and Large Language Model (LLM) processing.
- **Hugging Face / Open Source Models:** Used for emotion and sentiment detection.

*Note: Data sent to these third-party APIs is strictly used for real-time processing to generate responses. We do not sell your personal data or conversation history to data brokers or advertising networks.*

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Local/Cloud Storage:** Your session history, conversation logs, and account details are stored in a secure database (`SQLite` or equivalent production database). 
- **Authentication:** We use industry-standard JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for secure authentication and authorize your access to personal session histories.
- **Encryption:** Passwords are mathematically hashed and salted. We employ secure protocols to transmit your data over the internet.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Control

Since Mythri handles sensitive mental health interactions, you have the right to:
- **Access Your Data:** View your past consultation history directly through the application's interface.
- **Delete Your Data:** You can request the deletion of your account and all associated conversation histories at any time.
- **Opt-Out of Voice Processing:** You may choose to interact with Mythri strictly via text if you do not wish to use the voice processing features.

---

## 6. Medical Disclaimer

Mythri is an AI companion designed for emotional support and wellness. **It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or psychiatric treatment.** If you are experiencing a medical emergency or severe mental health crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a dedicated crisis hotline immediately. 

---

## 7. Academic and Research Collaboration

Mythri is actively collaborating with psychology researchers. However, any user data utilized for creating psychologically validated conversation corpuses, improving ICMR compliance, or joint research will be **strictly anonymized and de-identified** beforehand.

---

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time as our application evolves. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
